## 📋 WHAT GOES WHERE:

### **📂 1-WEBSITE**
**Purpose:** All live website files
**Files to put here:**
- ✅ index.html (current English version)
- ✅ index-es.html (current Spanish version)
- ✅ favicon.svg
- ✅ README.md (if you have it)

**Why separate:** These are your LIVE files. Never mess with these unless updating the actual website.

---

### **📂 2-MARKETING**
**Purpose:** All templates and marketing materials
**Files to put here:**
- ✅ business-cards-and-flyers.html
- ✅ cold-outreach-messages.md
- ✅ winning-offerup-post.md
- ✅ finding-real-customers-guide.md

**Why separate:** These are reference docs you'll use daily for messaging, posting, etc.

---

### **📂 3-TRACKING**
**Purpose:** Customer data and business metrics
**Files to put here:**
- ✅ glitch-tech-customer-tracker.xlsx

**Later add:**
- Invoice templates
- Customer records
- Revenue reports
- Job logs

**Why separate:** This is your business data - keep it clean and separate from marketing stuff.

---

### **📂 4-GUIDES**
**Purpose:** Setup instructions and how-to docs
**Files to put here:**
- ✅ analytics-setup-guide.md

**Later add:**
- Repair guides
- Troubleshooting docs
- Pricing guides
- Tool lists

**Why separate:** Reference materials you'll check occasionally but don't need daily.

---

### **📂 5-ARCHIVE**
**Purpose:** Old versions and backups
**Files to put here:**
- ❌ OLD index.html files you deleted
- ❌ Old drafts
- ❌ Replaced versions

**Why separate:** Keep old stuff just in case, but out of the way.
