🎨 Glitch Tech Business Cards & Flyers

How to Print:

  • Business Cards: Print on 8.5" x 11" cardstock, then cut along the borders. You'll get 10 cards per sheet.
  • Flyer: Print on 8.5" x 11" paper or cardstock. Post these at coffee shops, libraries, community centers, etc.

Where to Print:

  • Home Printer: Use cardstock paper (110lb recommended)
  • Print Shop: FedEx Office, Staples, or local print shop (~$10-20 for 100 business cards)
  • Online: Vistaprint, Moo, or GotPrint for professional quality
