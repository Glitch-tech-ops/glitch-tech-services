## 💡 PRO TIPS

### DO'S:
✅ Respond within 5-10 minutes of them posting (be FAST)
✅ Mention you're LOCAL (this is huge - people hate shipping)
✅ Always offer free diagnostic
✅ Be specific about turnaround time (same day/24hr)
✅ Mention your area (Hollywood/West Park/Hallandale)
✅ Keep it conversational and friendly
✅ Follow up once if no response (wait 24 hours)

### DON'TS:
❌ Don't be pushy or salesy
❌ Don't bad-mouth competitors (just focus on your value)
❌ Don't give exact prices until you see it (say "around $X")
❌ Don't spam - one message, one follow-up max
❌ Don't sound like a bot - personalize each message

## 🎯 WHAT TO LOOK FOR

### HIGH-PRIORITY POSTS (Message these FIRST):
- "Computer won't turn on"
- "Laptop running slow"
- "Virus/malware help"
- "Need computer fixed ASAP"
- "Data recovery"
- Posts with "$" in them (already thinking about budget)
- Posts in Spanish (less competition, you have Spanish site!)

### MEDIUM-PRIORITY:
- "Computer help needed"
- "Tech support"
- "PC issues"

### LOW-PRIORITY (Still message, but not urgent):
- "Looking for cheap computer" (they want to buy, not repair)
- Very old posts (7+ days)
